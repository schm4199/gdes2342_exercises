Roda Ahmed
Saturday, April 20th, 11AM

We are honored to welcome Roda Ahmed,
author of Mae Among the Stars, to Wild
Rumpus! Her beautiful picture book
about Mae Jemison, the first African
American woman to travel in space, has
received much well-deserved acclaim
and is a perfect book to inspire young
feminists, regardless of gender.

Roda Ahmed is a Norwegian author and
columnist born in Hargeisa, Somalia. Roda
is the bestselling author of Forberedelsen
(The Preparations). Mae Among the Stars
is her first children’s book. She can speak
five languages: Somali, Arabic, Norwegian,
English, and French. Roda lives in LA with
her husband and two children.
For more info check out www.rodaworld.com.
